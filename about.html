Our Story

Premium Craftsmanship Since 1998

Cooper Plumbing & Heating was founded by Director John Cooper with a simple promise: deliver exceptional plumbing and heating work at fair, transparent prices. What began as a one-man operation in Drogheda has grown into one of Co. Louth's most respected mechanical services companies — yet the founding values remain unchanged.

For over 25 years, John and his skilled team have served homeowners across Louth, Meath, and the wider community with a level of care and attention that larger firms simply cannot match. Every project — whether an emergency burst pipe at midnight or a full renewable energy upgrade — receives the same meticulous standards that have defined our work from day one.

Our reputation has been built entirely on word-of-mouth. We have never relied on flashy advertising or empty promises. Instead, satisfied customers have recommended us to their neighbours, families, and friends — and that trust is something we work hard to earn on every single job.

We specialise in tailored solutions for residential properties, taking the time to understand each home's unique requirements before recommending the right approach. From transparent pricing with no hidden fees to reliable scheduling and clean, professional finishes — Cooper Plumbing & Heating is the name local families turn to when quality matters.
